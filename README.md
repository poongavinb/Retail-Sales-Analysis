🚀 Retail Sales & Profitability Analytics Dashboard
📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes retail sales data from a Superstore dataset to uncover business insights related to sales performance, profitability, customer behavior, discount impact, and regional trends.

The project combines:

Python-based Data Analysis
Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
Business Intelligence & Visualization
Power BI Dashboarding

The goal is to help stakeholders understand:

Which categories and regions generate the most profit
How discounts impact profitability
Sales and profit trends over time
Top-performing products and sub-categories

📊 Key Business Insights
✅ Technology Category Generated Highest Profit

Technology products contributed the highest overall profit compared to Furniture and Office Supplies.

✅ High Discounts Reduced Profitability

Products with higher discounts showed significantly lower profit margins and even negative profits in some cases.

✅ West Region Performed Best

The West region generated the highest overall profit among all regions.

✅ Some Sub-Categories Were Loss-Making

Sub-categories like:

Tables
Bookcases
Supplies

showed negative profitability.

✅ Strong Seasonal Sales Trends

Sales and profit showed noticeable spikes during certain months, indicating seasonal purchasing behavior.
